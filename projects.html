Projects

Interactive work with policy, data, and ethics at the center.

These tools are built as teaching and thinking instruments, not just demos. Each tries to make a hard conceptual problem visible, manipulable, and intellectually honest.

Two tools

Both run entirely in the browser and focus on healthcare policy, algorithmic fairness, and decision-making under tradeoffs.

Health Policy

Health Policy Simulator

Explore how subsidies, mandates, reimbursement, and public program eligibility shift the balance between coverage, cost, and access.

Open simulator

Algorithmic Fairness

Algorithmic Bias Explorer

See how cost-based prediction can miss high-need patients and how fairness constraints trade off against accuracy in clinical allocation systems.
