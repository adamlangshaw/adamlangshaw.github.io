Influences

Books and thinkers that shaped how I think about systems, policy, and medicine.

  • Being Mortal Atul Gawande
    how medicine fails at the end of life
  • The Human Right to Health Jonathan Wolff
    the limits of rights-based frameworks in healthcare
  • A Theory of Justice John Rawls
    the original position and distributive fairness
  • Anarchy, State, and Utopia Robert Nozick
    the libertarian counterargument to Rawls
  • Outliers Malcolm Gladwell
    circumstance, opportunity, and the architecture of success
  • Democracy in America Alexis de Tocqueville
    civic association and the habits of democratic life
  • Justice: What's the Right Thing to Do? Michael Sandel
    competing moral frameworks applied to real policy
  • The Life You Can Save Peter Singer
    utilitarian ethics and the obligation to act
  • Thinking, Fast and Slow Daniel Kahneman
    heuristics, bias, and how judgment actually works
  • The Power Broker Robert Caro
    how institutions are built and captured
