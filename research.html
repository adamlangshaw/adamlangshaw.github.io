Journal of Cystic Fibrosis

The prevalence and clinical characteristics of immigrant people with cystic fibrosis at a single center in Miami, Florida

  • Published Journal of Cystic Fibrosis, supplement (2024)
  • Presented North American Cystic Fibrosis Conference, Boston — September 27, 2024
  • Institution University of Miami Miller School of Medicine

The study examined how immigrant status intersects with clinical outcomes and care access in cystic fibrosis patients at a major academic medical center in Miami, contributing to a growing body of work on health equity in rare disease populations. It is among the first to characterize the demographic and clinical profile of immigrant CF patients at a high-volume U.S. center.
